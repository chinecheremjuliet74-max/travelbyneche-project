❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about booking, payments, visas, insurance, and more.

📋 Booking 💳 Payment 🛂 Visa 🛡️ Insurance 📌 General
Booking How do I book a trip?

You can book a trip in three easy ways:

  • Online: Browse our packages and click "Book Now" on any listing.
  • Phone: Call us at +1 (555) 123-4567 and our team will assist you.
  • Email: Send your inquiry to hello@travelbyneche.com and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Once you complete your booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Payment What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods to make your booking convenient:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
  • Bank Transfers: Direct bank transfers are available for larger bookings.
  • Installment Plans: We offer flexible payment plans for select packages.

All payments are processed through our secure, encrypted payment gateway.

Visa Do I need a visa for my destination?

Visa requirements vary by destination and your country of citizenship. Here's what you need to know:

  • Visa-Free Travel: Many destinations offer visa-free entry for citizens of certain countries.
  • Visa on Arrival: Some countries allow you to obtain a visa upon arrival at the airport.
  • E-Visa: Many countries now offer electronic visas that can be applied for online.
  • Traditional Visa: Some destinations require you to apply at an embassy or consulate in advance.

We provide a visa guide for every package, and our team can assist you with the application process. Contact us for personalized support.

Insurance Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not automatically included in our packages, but we highly recommend purchasing it for your protection.

  • What it covers: Medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, flight delays, and more.
  • How to get it: We offer competitive insurance plans during the booking process. You can also purchase insurance independently.
  • Our recommendation: We partner with trusted insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage at affordable rates.

Contact us if you need help choosing the right plan for your trip.

General What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be flexible while protecting our operations:

  • 30+ days before departure: Full refund (minus administrative fees).
  • 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund.
  • 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund.
  • Less than 7 days: No refund, but we may offer credit toward a future booking.

Please note that some packages may have different policies. Check your booking confirmation for specific details.

General Can I make changes to my booking?

Yes, we understand that plans can change. Here's what you need to know:

  • Date Changes: Subject to availability. Fees may apply depending on how close to the departure date.
  • Package Changes: You can upgrade or downgrade your package, subject to availability and price differences.
  • Passenger Changes: Name changes are possible but may incur administrative fees.

Contact our support team as early as possible to make changes to your booking.

Booking Do you offer group discounts?

Absolutely! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more travelers.

  • 10-15 travelers: 10% discount on the total package price.
  • 16-25 travelers: 15% discount.
  • 26+ travelers: Custom pricing available upon request.

Contact our group booking specialist at groups@travelbyneche.com for a personalized quote.

Booking Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! We offer special discounts for students with a valid student ID.

  • Student Discount: Up to 15% off select packages.
  • Eligibility: Full-time students with a valid student ID or university email.
  • How to book: Contact our team directly to verify your student status and receive your discount.

We also offer budget-friendly packages specifically designed for student travelers.

Insurance Is it safe to travel with you?

Your safety is our top priority. Here's how we ensure a safe travel experience:

  • Vetted Partners: We carefully screen all hotels, transportation providers, and local guides.
  • Safety Protocols: All our tours follow strict safety guidelines and emergency procedures.
  • 24/7 Support: Our team is available around the clock to assist you during your trip.
  • Travel Advisories: We monitor travel advisories and will adjust itineraries if needed.

Read more about our safety commitment on our website.

We're here to help! Reach out to us through any of these channels:

  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567 (24/7 support)
  • Email: support@travelbyneche.com
  • Live Chat: Available on our website (click the chat icon in the bottom right corner).
